Travel

Planes

Ronald Reagan Washington National Airport
Google Maps

Reagan is the most convenient airport. You can easily take a taxi five miles from the Airport to any of the hotels.

Baltimore Washington International Airport
Google Maps

BWI is also a convenient airport, but will probably require a rental car. The drive will take approximately 50 minutes depending on traffic.

Dulles International Airport
Google Maps

Dulles is just as convenient as BWI. It will also require a rental car. The drive will take approximately 50 minutes depending on traffic.

Automobiles

From BWI and I-95 from the North
35 Miles
Map from BWI

Take I-95 S, I-495 S, Baltimore-Washington Pkwy and State Hwy 295 to Maine Ave SW in Washington. Take the Maine Ave exit from I-395 S. Take 15th St NW to Pennsylvania Ave NW.

From Pittsburgh and I-270 from the North
Map from
Pittsburgh

Take I-270 S and George Washington Memorial Pkwy to 12th St Expy in Washington. Take the 12th St exit from I-395 N. Continue on 12th St Expy. Drive to Pennsylvania Ave NW.

From Dulles International Airport
28 Miles
Map from
Dulles Airport

Continue on Dulles Access Rd. Take I-66 E to US-50 E/Constitution Ave NW in Washington. Exit from I-66 E. Continue on US-50 E/Constitution Ave NW. Drive to Pennsylvania Ave NW.

From Ronald Reagan Washington National Airport
5 Miles

Reagan Airport

Get on I-395 N from George Washington Memorial Pkwy. Continue on I-395 N. Take 12th St Expy to 12th St NW. Continue on 12th St NW. Drive to Pennsylvania Ave NW.
